History

Whitewater Rafting Company has been creating exciting river adventures since 1995. We pride ourselves on safety, professionalism, and unforgettable experiences for all our guests.

Over the years, we’ve expanded our routes, trained world-class guides, and welcomed thousands of happy rafters from all over the world.
